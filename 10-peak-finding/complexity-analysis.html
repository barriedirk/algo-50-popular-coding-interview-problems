Solution 2:

Time complexity: O(logn)

Explanation:

left and right start at the extremities of arr, but at each iteration, either left becomes equal to mid+1 or right becomes equal to mid, so we are dividing the number of remaining elements by 2, this is why the loop does at most log2_(n) iterations before left and right meet. log2_(n) because the number of divisions by 2 to go from n to 1 is log_2(n). And each iteration has an O(1) cost, so:

T(n) = log_2(n)*O(1) = O(logn)

Space complexity: O(1)

Explanation:

We're using 3 variables left, right, and mid, so:

S(n) = 3 = O(1)
